What is this?

A versatile, free, and highly customizale worksheet generator for simple addition, subtraction, and multiplication problems.

Why?

I teach a group of 3rd and 4th graders, and I just needed a simple workshet generator for additional enrichment.

I came across the originial repo in my search for an open-source alternative to Kuta worksheet software.

The existing repo gives me a great foundation to start from, and I plan to innovate on the existing features while adding new things to hopefully make this a tool that makes math worksheets more accessible to everyone.
